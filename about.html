About Us

Our Hotel

Residing near the naryani river, Hotel red roof has been providing its services from the last 5 years. It serves its customers with pride and dignity. It promises guests an unforgettable experience of comfort, care and compassionate service from the staff at an affordable rate. Enjoying the perfect river view, tharu cultural dance, jeep saffari,provides a spectacular jungle view along with the most beautiful sunset view. Red Roof is a well-established chain of economy hotels primarily located across the Naryanght. Red Roof hotels cater to budget-conscious travelers by offering clean, comfortable rooms equipped with features like flat-screen TVs and free Wi-Fi. Some locations also provide extras such as pet-friendly policies and complimentary breakfast options.

Our Mission

So it's obvious that Hotel Red Roof chain's main mission and vision is to be the most hospitable around the naryanghat chitwan by creating and delivering exceptional experiences to our guests. To achieve and make great guest experiences, hotels must have exceptional team members to do so.

Our vision

Hotel Red Roof is a Eco-friendly hotels, also known as Red Roof hotels or sustainable hotels, focus on minimizing their environmental impact while providing high-quality service to guests in both outdoor and Indoor activities.
