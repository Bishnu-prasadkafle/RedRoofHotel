Welcome To Hotel Red Roof

Red Roof is your Happy Place

Residing near the naryani river, Hotel red roof has been providing its services from the last 5 years. It serves its customers with pride and dignity. It promises guests an unforgettable experience of comfort, care and compassionate service from the staff at an affordable rate. Enjoying the perfect river view, tharu cultural dance, jeep saffari,provides a spectacular jungle view along with the most beautiful sunset view. Red Roof is a well-established chain of economy hotels primarily located across the Naryanght. Red Roof hotels cater to budget-conscious travelers by offering clean, comfortable rooms equipped with features like flat-screen TVs and free Wi-Fi. Some locations also provide extras such as pet-friendly policies and complimentary breakfast options. The chain continues to expand and renovate, maintaining its reputation for providing straightforward, economical lodging options for both business and leisure travelers seeking value without sacrificing comfort.
